Why study Latin?

In this day of computers and modern technology when there are an infinite number of things one can learn about, why elect to study a dead language? Logically, it makes sense for students of our generation to avoid antiquated passions like Latin because we’ve been told there isn’t a place for it in the job market. Yet we disagree. The idea behind offering Latin to students is to prove these ideologies wrong.

Latin club provides students with an understanding of the language, the culture associated with it, and the history of the Roman Empire. In doing so, participants will learn the building blocks of language, essential knowledge of the classics, and the evolution of human culture.
